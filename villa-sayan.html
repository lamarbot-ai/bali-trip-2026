Activities & Experiences

Campuhan Ridge Walk

Iconic ridge walk between two valleys. Best at sunrise for golden light and cooler temps. 2km easy walk.

Four Seasons Spa

World-class spa just 5 minutes away. Balinese massages, river-view treatment rooms. Book as a day guest.

Tirta Empul Temple

Sacred spring water temple. Participate in traditional purification ritual. Bring sarong and sash.

Ayung River Rafting

2-hour white water adventure through jungle gorge. Suitable for beginners. Pickup from Sayan.
